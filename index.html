Forward Propagation

Imagine you're teaching a friend how to bake a cake. You provide them with the recipe, they follow each step, and out comes a delicious cake (hopefully!). In a similar way, forward propagation in neural networks is like following a recipe: you take the inputs, mix them with weights and biases, apply activation functions, and out pops an output.

In this lesson, we'll explore the mathematical steps involved in forward propagation through a simple neural network. We'll break down each component so that you can understand how inputs are transformed into outputs.
